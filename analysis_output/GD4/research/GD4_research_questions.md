# GD4 Research Questions on Human-AI Relationships

- Relating to AI: How are people relating to / forming relationships with “AI”?
- Feelings about Own Relating: How do people feel about how they are relating to / their relationships with “AI”?
- Feelings about Others' Relating: How do people feel about how others are relating to / their relationships with “AI”?
- Social Fabric Change: How do people think “relationships (broadly) with AI” are changing the social fabric?
- What Should Be Done: What do people think should be done about:
    - How people are relating to AI (and vice versa)
    - The changing social fabric due to AI relationships (broadly)


Specific research questions that build on previous research:
- What boundaries are acceptable for AI in traditionally human relational roles, and which relational needs are seen as uniquely human?
- Will AI relationships strengthen or weaken human social connections, and what factors shape these beliefs?
- How do parents view the risks and benefits of children forming close relationships with AI companions?
- How do people distinguish between functional AI versus companionship AI, and what are the ethical lines when AI encourages deeper relationships?
- For older adults, what are the trade-offs between AI companionship benefits versus concerns about dependency and exploitation?
